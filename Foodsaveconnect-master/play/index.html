How It Works

Three simple steps to rescue surplus food and deliver it to communities in need.
Every meal saved is a life touched.

1. List Surplus Food

Restaurants, caterers, and individuals post their surplus food with details like quantity, preparation time, and expiry. It takes less than a minute.

2. Scout Needy Locations

Volunteer scouts identify and report locations where food is needed most -- shelters, street communities, elderly homes -- pinning them on our live map.

3. Deliver & Verify

Scouts claim delivery tasks, pick up surplus food, and deliver it to needy locations. Photo proof ensures transparency and accountability.

Platform Features

A technology-driven approach to food rescue with real-time coordination,
smart matching, and complete transparency.

Live Map Dashboard

Interactive map with clustered markers showing food listings, needy locations, and active deliveries in real time.

Smart Expiry Tracking

Locations auto-expire after 4 hours to keep data fresh. Scouts can extend active locations with one tap.

Delivery Verification

Before-and-after photo proof for every delivery ensures full accountability and builds community trust.

Gamification & Badges

Earn karma points, unlock badges, and climb ranks from "Rising Scout" to "Food Hero" as you contribute.

Our Mission: Zero Food Waste

Every day, tonnes of perfectly good food goes to waste while millions go hungry. FoodSaver Connect is a real-time coordination platform that matches surplus food with communities in need -- powered by a network of volunteer scouts who bridge the last mile.
